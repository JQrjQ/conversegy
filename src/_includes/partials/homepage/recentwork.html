George Murphy

Singer/Songwriter

The George Murphy Music website was created by the team at Conversegy to facilitate the promotion of George's music and his band, The Rising Sons.

An e-commerce store was enabled allowing his team to manage products, take orders and accept online payments from an intuitive back end system.

A user friendy CMS (Content Management System) was developed allowing certain parts of the site to be edited.(Upcoming gigs, latest news)
